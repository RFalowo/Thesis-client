Practise Round - Sound Design
To familiarise you with the experimental procedure we will first go through a practise round whereby your responses will not be recorded.
In the first practise round you will be a sound designer. You will be presented with a 3D object and your task is to design a sound that you think matches the object. Please try to take into every aspect, including the colour. Do not worry, there are no wrong answers just select the values of the sound parameters that you think work best! All of the controls wil be in the top right of the window and you can use your mouse to rotate and zoom. Once you are happy with your choices, press the 'Submit' button at the bottom of the controls.
Practise Round - 3D Design
In this practise round you will take on the role of a 3D designer. Your task is to do opposite of the previous, listen to the sound being played at regular intervals and try to create a 3D object that matches the sound using the controls. Once you are happy with your object, press the 'Submit' button.
Trial 1
Now you're all set! The experiment will consist of 5 rounds of sound design and 5 rounds of 3D design. Do not worry if you can't finish the experiment in one sitting, your progress will be saved. After you have finished the study there will be short questionnaire followed by a chance to enter into a prize draw. Good luck and have fun!
